Products

All cans produced at ZPI Dneprovskyi undergo rigorous quality control. Tin sheets are cut into the necessary blanks, which are then subjected to the stamping process. This process involves pressing the material to form the body of the can. The blank passes through several presses, gradually forming a cylindrical shape. The body seams are welded to ensure airtightness. Inside, the seam is coated with powder and liquid lacquer to prevent corrosion. After assembly, the can is sterilized. Its airtightness and resistance to external influences are thoroughly tested. At the customer's request, lithography with logos and other markings is applied to the exterior of the can using special printers, giving the can a unique style and informing the consumer. The production of tin cans at ZPI Dneprovskyi requires high precision and control at every stage to ensure the quality and safety of the product, as well as compliance with industry standards and requirements.

About the Company

Since 2008

Welcome to the website of Dneprovsky Industrial Products Plant LLC! We are pleased to introduce you to our plant, a leading manufacturer of high-quality tin cans for the production of canned food. Our company was founded in 2008 in the city of Dnipro, Ukraine.

At our facility, located in the heart of Ukraine, we produce tin cans that are of high quality due to the special lacquer coating on both sides. In addition, our cans are lithographed, which ensures not only durability but also aesthetic appeal.
